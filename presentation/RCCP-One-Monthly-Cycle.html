Monthly RCCP cycle — who does what, when

Eight working days, four phases, seven departments. Same rhythm every month so everyone knows their slot.

1
WD 1 → 3 · Inputs

Collect & refresh inputs

  • MFG exception templates returned — line capacity (PM, shutdowns) + headcount (leave, sickness)
  • S&OP demand forecast (PIR) refreshed for the 12-month horizon
  • PRODUCT new launches confirmed — SKU, line, effective month
  • SAP exports pulled — COOIS (orders), MB51 (actuals), MB52 (stock)
→
2
WD 4 → 5 · Build

Build & validate the batch

  • PLAN fold inputs into the 6 source files — headcount Sheet 3, capacity calendar, portfolio changes
  • Create new batch in RCCP One · upload all 6 files
  • 7-stage validation — fix anything BLOCKED at source, re-upload
  • Publish candidate · open the dashboard · sanity-check yourself · capture talking points
→
3
WD 6 → 8 · Review

Pre-read & RCCP meeting

  • PLAN share PDF + Excel data pack 24-48h before — pre-read for stakeholders
  • ELT + MFG + S&OP + FIN attend the RCCP review meeting
  • Walk the dashboard — Action Items → Capacity charts → People fit detail
  • Capture decisions live · tick Action Items as they land
→
4
WD 8 → MO-end · Sign-off

Close the loop & lock baseline

  • If inputs change as a result — correct source → re-validate → re-publish
  • ELT approval captured against the batch (date, who, what)
  • Baseline lock — the agreed plan for the cycle, immutable, auditable
  • PLAN follows up next cycle — did approved actions actually happen?
